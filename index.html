Our Story

Indian Chowk began as a small food stall at the city market decades ago. Passed down through two generations, our recipes focus on seasonal produce, slow-cooked gravies, and traditional spice blends. We remain faithful to methods that honor the past: stone-ground spices, hand-rolled breads and a simple hospitality that makes every guest feel at home.
